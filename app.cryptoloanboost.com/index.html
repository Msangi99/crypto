About

Why CryptoLoanBoost

CryptoLoanBoost (CLB) is a decentralized finance protocol built on the Binance Smart Chain that bridges the gap between traditional lending and leveraged crypto investing. By depositing BNB into one of our staking pools, you gain access to instant leveraged loans denominated in CLB tokens, allowing you to amplify your exposure to Bitcoin and Ethereum price movements without selling your underlying collateral.

The protocol operates through a series of smart contracts that automatically manage collateral, issue loans, and execute liquidations when target prices are reached. Every deposit is secured on-chain and backed by a soulbound BEP-20 receipt token that represents your position. Whether the market moves up or down, your original deposit remains protected, and profits are distributed transparently between you and the protocol.

Our tiered leverage system is designed to reward commitment. Starting from just $100, you can unlock 10x leverage, scaling all the way up to 60x leverage at the $1,000 tier. This structure ensures that both small and large investors can participate proportionally. The CLB token serves as the native currency of the ecosystem, used for loan issuance, fee payments, and reward distribution across our 5-level referral program.

Security and transparency are at the core of everything we build. All transactions are recorded on the BSC blockchain and verifiable through BscScan. The protocol's auto-liquidation engine runs continuously, monitoring BTC and ETH prices in real-time to ensure positions are settled fairly when targets are hit. With wallet-based authentication, PIN protection, and biometric unlock options in our mobile app, your assets and data remain under your control at all times.

Token

The CLB Coin

The CryptoLoanBoost Coin (CLB) is the native utility token powering the entire CLB ecosystem. Designed as a BEP-20 asset on the Binance Smart Chain, CLB is used to issue leveraged loans, distribute referral rewards, pay protocol fees, and govern future upgrades. Every deposit into a CLB pool automatically mints receipt tokens that represent your share of the pool and unlock proportional leverage.

CLB holders benefit from multiple revenue streams: staking rewards from pool APYs, commissions from the 5-level referral tree, and profit shares from auto-liquidated positions. The token's deflationary mechanics ensure that a portion of every fee is burned, creating long-term scarcity while rewarding active participants.

With a fixed total supply and transparent on-chain economics, CLB represents more than just a utility token — it is your key to accessing institutional-grade leveraged crypto products, backed by the speed and low costs of the BSC network. Trade it, stake it, or simply hold it to grow your share of the protocol's success.
