Mechanics

How CLB works

1

Deposit & lock

Stake BNB into a pool. Your deposit backs your position and mints a soulbound BEP-20 receipt on BSC.

2

Leveraged loan

CLB loans scale with your tier — higher commitment can mean higher leverage in CLB terms.

3

Auto-liquidation

When BTC or ETH hit protocol targets, positions can auto-liquidate in phases; a large share of profits is designed to return to you.

About

CryptoLoanBoost

Leveraged exposure on BSC. CLB combines pooled deposits, CLB-denominated loans, and a multi-level referral program. The mobile app is where you open pools, manage security (PIN / biometrics), and move funds end-to-end.
CLB token (BEP-20) is used across the ecosystem for fees, rewards, and loan mechanics. This web page is only your read-only mirror after you connect — it does not replace the app for deposits or sensitive recovery flows.
